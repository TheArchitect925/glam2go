# UI Principles

## Fast Booking
- reduce hesitation between discovery and booking
- keep decision points obvious

## Low-Friction Discovery
- show enough information to compare artists quickly
- do not bury core filters or price signals

## Trust-First Design
- portfolio, pricing clarity, travel policy, and booking confirmation are trust surfaces
- ambiguous fees and vague package copy damage conversion

## Elegant But Readable
- premium does not mean low contrast or tiny text
- beauty presentation should still feel operationally clear

## Visual Consistency
- shared component behavior should stay consistent across screens
- avoid one-off spacing, border, or shadow experiments

## Clear Pricing And Service Details
- show what is included
- show base price and distance/travel adjustments clearly
- do not surprise users late in the flow

## Respectful Distance And Travel Fees
- explain extra travel cost plainly
- avoid making location-related pricing feel punitive or hidden
