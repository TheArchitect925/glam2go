# Product Vision

## What Glam2GO Is
Glam2GO is a mobile marketplace for booking makeup artists for events, occasions, and personal beauty appointments. The product should feel premium, reliable, and fast, with the booking flow carrying the same clarity users expect from on-demand services.

## Core Value Proposition
- Customers can find the right artist quickly.
- Artists can present their work, pricing, and travel policy clearly.
- Booking should feel low-friction and trustworthy.

## Primary Users
- Customers booking for weddings, parties, shoots, graduations, and special occasions
- Makeup artists managing services, pricing, radius, and portfolio

## Marketplace Concept
- Demand side: customers browse and book
- Supply side: artists publish availability, packages, travel policy, and portfolio
- Platform side: quality, trust, and future moderation

## Trust Factors
- visible pricing and service inclusions
- clear travel and distance fees
- strong portfolio presentation
- review surface later
- explicit booking status and confirmation

## Product Tone
- premium but readable
- beauty-service aesthetic without clutter
- calm, polished, and operationally clear
