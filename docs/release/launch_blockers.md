# Launch Blockers

## Public Launch Blockers
- Customer account APIs are still local-only for favorites, saved addresses, profile, and notification preferences.
- Final legal and policy copy is still draft content.
- Notification delivery, analytics, and crash-reporting vendors are not connected.
- Portfolio media upload and storage are not implemented.

## Conditional External-Beta Blockers
- Remote environment flags must match the target backend.
- Debug default accounts must remain disabled.
- Beta guidance must clearly state that some account-management sync and notification delivery capabilities are limited.

## Non-Blocking But Visible Risks
- Support flow still lacks a final direct contact destination.
- Advanced cancellation enforcement and rescheduling remain out of scope.
- Media-related artist portfolio expectations must stay tightly scoped in copy and tester guidance.
