# Launch Checklist Master

## Launch-Ready
- Shared role-aware app shell
- Session bootstrap and protected-action continuation
- Discovery and artist-profile read paths
- Booking request lifecycle across customer and artist surfaces
- Artist operational setup surfaces
- Support and policy route structure

## Conditional-Launch-Ready
- External beta with remote auth, discovery, bookings, and artist management enabled
- Portfolio metadata management without full media upload
- Notification readiness state without push transport
- Analytics and crash boundaries without production vendor hookup

## Blocked For Public Launch
- Remote favorites, saved addresses, customer profile, and preference sync
- Final legal content approval
- Production notification, analytics, and crash vendor hookup
- Portfolio media upload and storage
