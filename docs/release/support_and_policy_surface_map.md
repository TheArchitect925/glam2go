# Support And Policy Surface Map

## Shared Support Hub
- route: `/support`
- entry points:
  - customer settings
  - artist settings
  - booking detail context via booking policy CTA

## Policy Detail Routes
- `/support/privacy`
- `/support/terms`
- `/support/cancellation`
- `/support/booking-policy`

## Release Rule
- keep policy content centralized under the support route group
- do not duplicate legal or booking-policy copy across multiple settings screens
- keep support contact guidance centralized as well; if a final contact method is added, do not scatter it across unrelated feature screens

## Current Gap
- the support hub currently provides guidance and policy entry, but still needs a finalized direct support destination before public launch
